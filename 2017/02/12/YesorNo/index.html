YesorNo

A bad bush is better than the open field.
Better master one than engage with ten.
Books, like friends, should be few and well chosen.
Do business, but be not a slave to it.
Debt is better than death.
Do not judge by appearances.
Do not look upon the vessel, but upon that which it contains.
Do not speak all you know, but know all you speak.
Do nothing you would wish to conceal.
Fear not the future;weep not for the past.
He is unfortunate who can not bear misfortune.
Hate to see things done by halves. If it be right,do it boldly,if it be wrong,leave it undone.
If you can not have the best,make the best of what you have.
