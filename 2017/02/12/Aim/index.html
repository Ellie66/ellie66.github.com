Aim

A good archer is not known by his arrows but his aim.
A rose by any other name would smell as sweet.
A word is enough to the wise.
An ill tongue may do much.
A word spoken is an arrow let fly.
Action is the proper fruit of knowledge.
Be able to be alone. Lose not the advantage of solitude.
Better to sail slowly than not to sail at all.
Choice of the end covers choice of the means.
Custom without reason is but ancient error.
Deliberate before you begin, then execute with vigor.
Early sow,early mow.
Every man has his taste.
